Welcome to my website!

My name is Klaas De Cock, 36-year old Java Developer, born and raised in the Westhoek, nowadays living in Kontich. I am excited to start my new job at SPF Finances and to meet all of you! On this website, you can find some information about myself, some cocktail recipes and the link to my GitHub repository.

Playing bass at sea

For the last 6 years, I have been working as a session musician on cruiseships. I started of as double bass player in a jazz quartet. Later on, I took some gigs in theatre orchestras and pop bands too. This job has taken me to beautiful places and I am grateful to have met people from all around the world.

New career as developer

But after 1452 days sharing a tiny cabin and playing 'Besame Mucho' every night, I decided to move on. Two years ago, I got into coding, so when I came back in May 2020 from my last contract at sea, I signed up for a course Java Development at Realdolmen, organised by VDAB. I just graduated last week, so I am happy I can start right away with this new challenge at SPF Finances.
